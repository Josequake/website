Know Us !

History

We are a company that was born from a friendship forged in the United States between two Costa Ricans and an American. During their time together, they discovered the need to import high quality products to Costa Rica. Thus, we created Tico International, a public limited company dedicated to bringing innovative materials and technology from the United States to our native country. With effort and dedication, we established relationships with suppliers and are committed to offering the best to our customers. Despite initial challenges, our passion and collaboration allowed us to overcome obstacles. Today, Tico Internacional not only improves the quality of life of Costa Ricans, but also symbolizes the union of two cultures that dared to dream and build together.

How we work

At Tico Internacional, we facilitate the importation of products from the United States to Costa Rica efficiently. The customer makes his purchase, for example, on Amazon, and must send the package to a specific address provided by our company. Once the package arrives at our office, we provide you with an estimate of the shipping cost. It is important to note that the cost is not based on the weight of the package, but on its size. In this way, we guarantee a transparent service adapted to the needs of our clients.
